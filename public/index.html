Welcome to Rizz like a PRO-grammer!

Rizz like a PRO-grammer is a website designed to help you ask someone out in a cute nerdish romantic way.

Instructions:

  1. Choose the gender of the person you want to ask out
  1. On the next page after selecting a gender, use the "ASK" button to share the link to the question to your person of interest.
  2. Remember to let the person know to click a response and use the "SHARE" button to send the link to their response back to you

Happy Rizzing!!
